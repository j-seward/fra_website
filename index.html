What We Do

Our team works to form long-term relationships with partners across diverse sectors in order to identify and address our partners' most pressing concerns. We leverage our expertise to evaluate programs and policies as well as advise our partners based on data-driven evidence. Continued collaboration over time allows us to develop and refine actionable evidence-based insights to guide partners' decisions on resource allocation.

Who We Are

The "Four" in Four Rivers Analytics represents the four Founders and "Rivers" represents our ethos. Moving water has long been representative of life, and we aim to seek the flourishing of life using our analytical training as health economists, data scientists, and econometricians.
We also appreciate the similarities between rivers and data. Just as rivers are ever moving around and adapting to the obstacles in the land, that’s how we view data analytics and research. It’s an ever-changing landscape that needs to be constantly reevaluated and analyzed.
